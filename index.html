Frequently asked Questions

To address common queries from our visitors. Here are some frequently asked questions and their corresponding answers:

  • What types of photography services do you offer?

    We specialize in a wide range of photography services, including portrait photography, event coverage, corporate headshots, and more. Check out our "Services" page for a detailed list.

  • How long does it take to receive the edited photos after a session?

    The turnaround time for edited photos depends on the type of session. Typically, you can expect to receive a preview within 1-2 days, and the final edited photos within 2-3 days. We strive for quality and attention to detail in every edit.

  • What should I wear for a portrait session?

    We recommend wearing outfits that make you feel comfortable and confident. Solid colors work well, and it's a good idea to avoid busy patterns that may distract from the main subject. Feel free to bring multiple outfits for variety!

  • Do you travel for destination photo shoots?

    Absolutely! We love capturing moments in new and exciting locations. Contact us with the details of your destination, and we'll give you the best.

  • What equipment do you use for your photography?

    We use professional-grade camera equipment and lenses to ensure the highest quality images. Our gear is regularly maintained and updated to stay current with industry standards.
